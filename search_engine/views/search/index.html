mission

Every year the number of scientific publications grows exponentially and it becomes much more difficult to find the necessary information in such a voluminous array of knowledge. Researchers spend a significant amount of time reading and processing information whereas students find it difficult to gather scientific sources for their papers. As a result, practitioners prefer speculative conclusions, neglecting the scientific base that is difficult to handle. With the aim of solving these problems, Architexts has developed an innovative system for intelligent search of scientific publications in the field of architecture. 

Our development is an innovative search platform with the introduction of Artificial Intelligence. Users will be able to easily isolate the necessary information from hundreds of thousands of texts without wasting time processing unnecessary data.

The search and processing of information will become much more efficient, and the final content will be of better quality.

Our technology

Architexts uses advanced technologies for structuring and development. Gradient boosting technology allows efficient search. Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers technologies are used in article processing. Front-end development is based on Framework React and JavaScript.
