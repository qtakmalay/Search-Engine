Architexts'

mission

Our product is an innovative search platform for scientific articles and publications, designed specifically for researchers in the field of architecture. Every year there are more and more scientific articles, and researchers spend millions of hours reading publications and processing information.

Thanks to artificial intelligence, the platform provides a variation of the exact answers to the query, unlike conventional search engines. Now users can easily find the necessary information from publications and immediately apply them in their scientific papers.

Collecting information for scientific articles will become much easier, and the resulting content will be much better. This is an evolution in the approach to writing scientific articles and publications.

Our technology

Architexts uses cutting edge technology for structuring and development. Efficient search helps to implement Gradient boosting technology. For article preprocessing, we use Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers. Front-end development uses Framework React and JavaScript.
